**Design Movement**: Romantic Maximalism

**Core Principles**:
- Emotional Abundance: Embrace rich, layered visuals that evoke warmth and affection
- Playful Elegance: Balance sophisticated typography with whimsical interactive elements
- Tactile Depth: Use soft shadows, gradients, and blur to create a dreamy, touchable quality
- Joyful Motion: Animations should feel bouncy, alive, and celebratory

**Color Philosophy**: 
Soft romantic palette anchored by dusty rose (#E8B4B8), warm coral (#FF6B7A), and creamy ivory (#FFF8F0). Accents of deep burgundy (#8B2635) for contrast. The intent is to evoke the feeling of handwritten love letters and vintage Valentine cards—nostalgic yet fresh.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Centered emotional focal point with asymmetric decorative elements. The main question occupies the heart of the screen, while floating hearts, sparkles, and abstract shapes orbit around it, creating visual interest without rigid grid constraints.

**Signature Elements**:
- Floating animated hearts with varying sizes and opacities
- Soft glow effects around interactive elements
- Hand-drawn style illustrations and decorative flourishes

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Every interaction should feel rewarding and delightful. The "No" button's evasive behavior adds playful tension, while the "Yes" button remains steady and inviting—a metaphor for choosing love. Upon acceptance, the celebration should feel explosive yet tender.

**Animation**:
- Entrance: Gentle fade-in with subtle scale-up (0.95 → 1) using ease-out curves
- Button hover: Soft lift with shadow expansion, 150ms transition
- "No" button evasion: Smooth position transition with spring physics (tension: 300, friction: 20)
- Acceptance celebration: Confetti burst, heart explosions radiating outward, scale pulse on text
- Background elements: Slow floating motion (3-5s duration) with slight rotation

**Typography System**:
- Display: "Playfair Display" (serif) for the main question—romantic and timeless
- Body/Buttons: "Quicksand" (rounded sans-serif) for approachable, friendly feel
- Hierarchy: Display at 48-64px, buttons at 18-20px with medium weight

Electric magenta (#FF0080), pure white (#FFFFFF), and deep black (#0A0A0A) create maximum contrast. A touch of bright yellow (#FFD700) for celebratory accents. The intent is to make love feel modern, direct, and unafraid—like a bold declaration rather than a whisper.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Brutalist card system—the question lives in a thick-bordered, slightly rotated card floating in space. Asymmetric placement (off-center) with generous negative space. No traditional grid; elements are positioned with intentional imbalance.

**Signature Elements**:
- Thick (4-8px) black borders on all interactive elements
- Heavy drop shadows (8-12px offset) for depth
- Geometric heart shapes (angular, not curved)

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions should feel immediate and honest. The "No" button doesn't just move—it snaps away with purpose. The "Yes" button grows bolder on hover, demanding attention. Acceptance triggers a raw, energetic celebration with geometric confetti and bold typography animations.

**Animation**:
- Entrance: Instant appearance with slight rotation wobble (±2deg)
- Button hover: Instant scale (1.05x) with no easing—direct feedback
- "No" button evasion: Snap to new position (100ms cubic-bezier) with slight rotation
- Acceptance celebration: Geometric shapes explode outward, bold text scales up dramatically
- Background: Static or minimal motion—let the interactions do the talking

**Typography System**:
- Display: "Space Grotesk" (geometric sans) for the question—modern and bold
- Body/Buttons: "DM Sans" (neutral sans) for clarity
